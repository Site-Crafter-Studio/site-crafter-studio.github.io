Stand out in the job market


By the end of the tutorial, users will have a fully functional portfolio website that they can use to showcase your work to potential clients or employers. In addition, they will have the confidence to continue building your skills and creating more complex websites in the future.

The job market is becoming increasingly competitive, and having a self-coded portfolio website can help job seekers demonstrate their skills and expertise to potential employers. This is especially true for creative professionals such as designers, artists, and writers, who need a platform to showcase their work.
