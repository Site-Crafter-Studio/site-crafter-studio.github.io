Develop a new skillset


This walkthrough is intended for all types of early career professionals, including students with no prior coding experience. Throughout the guided tutorial stages, users will learn the basics of HTML and CSS concepts, developing new skills in front-end web development. In addition, they will have the confidence and familiary to continue making their website more complex and creative.

Stand out in the job market


By the end of the tutorial, users will have a fully functional portfolio website that they can use to showcase their work to potential clients or employers. The job market is becoming increasingly competitive and having a self-coded portfolio website can help job seekers demonstrate their skills and expertise to potential employers. This is especially true for creative professionals such as designers, artists, coders, and writers who need a platform to showcase their work.
